Vobiscum Satanas (Latin for "May Satan Be with You") is Dark Funeral's second full-length album. This was the first album to feature vocalist Emperor Magus Caligula, and the only one to feature drummer Alzazmon (Tomas Asklund), who would later be renowned for his work in Dissection and Gorgoroth.

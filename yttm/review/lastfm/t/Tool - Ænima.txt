Ænima (pronounced /ˈɒnɪmə/) is the second studio album by progressive metal band Tool.  (The 13th track, however, is called Ænema.)

The album was released on October 1, 1996 in Compact Disc format and in vinyl format on September 17, 1996. The album was recorded and cut at Ocean Way, Hollywood, California and The Hook, North Hollywood, California from 1995 to 1996. It was certified triple platinum by the RIAA on March 4, 2003. The album won the Grammy Award for Best Metal Performance in 1998.
